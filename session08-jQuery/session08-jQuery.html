The pains of JavaScript in the browser

JS is a fine language. Until you actually use it to build stuff in browsers.

Browsers tend to get inconsistent. Like, all the time.

On top of that, some browser APIs are too verbose and complicated for us mortals.

Meet the worst

  • DOM
  • Events
  • AJAX
  • Animations

Something must be done!

Yeah, well... Meet jQuery.

A rich, powerful JavaScript library, intended to simplify common tasks in the browser.

A bit of history

Created by John Resig in 2006

Free and open source, active thanks to a huge active community.

It's the most widespread JS library in use today. over 60% of the top 10,000 sites use jQuery.
